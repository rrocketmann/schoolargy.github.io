🔔 Platform Notice
Why Hollow Knight & Undertale Are Not Available
Hi everyone — we wanted to be transparent about why certain requested games are missing from the Groups section.

Hollow Knight and Undertale are commercial titles owned by Team Cherry and Toby Fox respectively. Neither game has an official browser version, and embedding or redistributing them without a license would violate copyright law. Any third-party sites claiming to host the full games are running unauthorized copies or fan remakes — we will not link to these.

We have added Bad Time Simulator as a fan-made Undertale alternative that is freely and legally available in the browser. We are continuing to look for legitimate browser-playable content to add to the platform.

If you have suggestions for games you'd like to see added, please reach out to the team below. Thank you for understanding.
